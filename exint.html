Intranet Use Cases

Internal Communication and Collaboration:

Corporate Announcements: Sharing company news, updates, and policy changes.

Team Collaboration: Using tools like Microsoft SharePoint or Slack for internal team discussions and project management.

Document Management:

Centralized Repository: Storing and managing internal documents, policies, and procedures.

Version Control: Keeping track of document revisions and ensuring employees access the latest versions.

Employee Self-Service:

HR Portals: Allowing employees to access payroll, benefits information, and submit leave requests.

Training and Development: Providing access to training materials, courses, and professional development resources.

Project Management:

Task Assignment: Assigning and tracking tasks within a project.

Resource Planning: Managing resources and timelines for projects.

Internal Social Networks:

Employee Engagement: Creating forums or social networks for employees to share ideas and connect.

Recognition Programs: Highlighting employee achievements and encouraging peer recognition.

Internet Use Cases

Marketing and Sales:

E-commerce Platforms: Selling products or services online through websites like Shopify or Amazon.

Digital Marketing: Using social media, SEO, and online advertising to reach potential customers.

Customer Service:

Support Portals: Providing customers with access to FAQs, support tickets, and live chat.

Feedback Systems: Collecting customer feedback through surveys and reviews.

Remote Work:

Cloud Services: Utilizing platforms like Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 for document sharing and collaboration.

Video Conferencing: Conducting meetings using tools like Zoom or Microsoft Teams.

Supply Chain Management:

Vendor Portals: Managing relationships with suppliers and tracking orders.

Inventory Management: Using online systems to monitor stock levels and automate reordering.

Data Analytics:

Web Analytics: Analyzing website traffic and user behavior using tools like Google Analytics.

Business Intelligence: Leveraging internet-based BI tools to gather insights from various data sources.
